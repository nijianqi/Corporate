Company Profile

Zhejiang Baijiahui Network Technology Co., Ltd. (abbreviated as Baijiahui Network Technology) was established in 2017. The core team of the company consists of a group of senior professionals with backgrounds in the Internet, international trade, security payments, and laws. The company is headquartered in beautiful Hangzhou, China. It has its own office building in Hangzhou Future Technology City. Currently, it has established the Zhejiang Central Operation Center in Lanxi, Zhejiang.

Based on the actual experience and achievements of foreign trade over the past two decades, Baijiahui Network Technology has already built and put into operation the key project “China Foreign Trade Home” platform. The platform is positioned as the leader of the global trade B2B trading service platform and aims to help the global buyers and the best suppliers in China achieve long-term and in-depth cooperation through the platform's two-way trading service. It includes a supplier optimization platform, an overseas promotion operation platform, and a foreign trade transaction service platform. "China's foreign trade homes" is the mission to "make the world do not do business", so that Chinese manufacturing companies can more easily and efficiently participate in global procurement and reach the target market.

For the future, the company will uphold The values ​​of “Openness, Integrity, Responsibility, and Growth” strive to create a brand-new model in the global trade B2B field, promote the development of China’s Internet industry with outstanding market foresight and technological innovation, and achieve a new future in international trade.
